1. Introduction

Welcome to Dabbo ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information when you register and use our App:

  • Name: To personalize your experience and for order processing
  • Email Address: For account verification, order confirmations, and communication
  • Mobile Number: For account authentication, order updates, and customer support
  • Password: Securely hashed and stored for account authentication

2.2 Profile Information

  • Profile Picture: Optional profile photo uploaded by you
  • Delivery Addresses: Name, address line, city, state, pincode, and phone number for order delivery

2.3 Order Information

  • Order History: Products purchased, quantities, prices, order dates
  • Payment Information: Payment method (currently Cash on Delivery), order totals
  • Shipping Information: Delivery addresses used for orders

2.4 Device Information

  • Device Permissions:
    • Camera: For taking profile pictures
    • Storage/Media: For selecting images from your device gallery
  • Device Type: Platform information (Android/iOS) for app optimization

2.5 Usage Information

  • Cart Data: Products added to shopping cart
  • App Activity: Features used, pages visited (for improving user experience)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

3.1 Account Management

  • Create and manage your user account
  • Authenticate your identity
  • Provide customer support

3.2 Order Processing

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Send order confirmations and updates
  • Manage delivery addresses
  • Handle returns and refunds (if applicable)

3.3 Communication

  • Send order-related notifications
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Send important app updates and policy changes

3.4 App Improvement

  • Analyze app usage to improve functionality
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Enhance user experience

3.5 Legal Compliance

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights and prevent fraud
  • Enforce our terms of service

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 Service Providers

We may share your information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf:

  • Cloudinary: For storing and managing profile pictures and product images
  • Backend Hosting Services: For storing your account and order data securely
  • Payment Processors: Currently not applicable (COD only), but may be used in future for online payments

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4.4 We Do NOT Sell Your Data
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Data Storage

  • Your data is stored on secure servers
  • Profile pictures are stored on Cloudinary's secure cloud storage
  • Order and account data is stored in encrypted databases

5.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Passwords are hashed using industry-standard encryption
  • Secure Transmission: All data transmission uses HTTPS/SSL encryption
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Security Audits: We regularly review and update our security practices

5.3 Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Order Data: Retained for legal and business purposes (typically 7 years for tax/accounting purposes)
  • Profile Pictures: Retained until you delete your account or update your profile picture

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

6.1 Access and Update

  • View and update your profile information through the App
  • Access your order history at any time
  • Update your delivery addresses

6.2 Account Deletion

  • Request deletion of your account by contacting us
  • Note: Some information may be retained for legal compliance (e.g., order records for tax purposes)

6.3 Permission Controls

  • Camera Permission: You can revoke camera permission in your device settings
  • Storage Permission: You can revoke storage permission in your device settings
  • Note: Revoking permissions may limit certain app features

6.4 Data Portability

  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Contact us at app.bspl@gmail.com to make this request
